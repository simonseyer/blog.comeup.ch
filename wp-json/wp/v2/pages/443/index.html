Dominical is a small surfer town located at the pacific ocean in the south of the country. You take off at the mountain ridge set back from the coast and fly over the jungle before landing at either Dominicalito or Dominical beach. Because of the consistent thermals at the takeoff ridge, it is easy to get and stay up and have fun close to the takeoff. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

If you are more into XC flying, you can follow the ridge to the north and south and make quite some distance. You’ve got a good takeoff altitude of 630 meters and are ensured spectacular views.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
