We went to Rivas with Alex, a Costa Rican tandem pilot that we met in Dominical. Driving the car was Timo Schuhmacher, a German rally driver<\/s>, sorry, exceptional paragliding, test, and tandem pilot<\/a>. At Alex’s place<\/a>, we felt very welcomed and at ease having a nice room with a little balcony and a wonderful view all for ourselves.
