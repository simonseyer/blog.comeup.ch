First, to embark on a traveling and paragliding sabbatical in South America. That’s the journey that we want to share with you in this blog. Second? Let’s see… \ud83d\ude42<\/p>\n\n\n\n

That’s not where we met, though, but at a local metal concert with barely any other visitors. That was in 2018. The same year we traveled to Washington together and started to learn paragliding.
