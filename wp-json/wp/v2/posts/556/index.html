In comparison to Dominical, which was a nice little surfer town with charm, we encountered Jac\u00f3 as a huge, noisy, party-surfer town with one hotel and shop after the other. We only stayed one night until we boarded a night bus to Panam\u00e0 the next evening. We made good use of our one day in Jac\u00f3, though, and went paragliding.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Our plan was to hike to the takeoff. We checked it on the map and it seemed to take around one hour. What we didn’t expect was the heat and blazing sun with near to no shade, the dry dusty slope, and the inaccurate mapping of roads on Google maps. We missed the path up way several times and had to turn back again.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
