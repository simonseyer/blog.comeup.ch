    Panam\u00e1 is where we started our journey. We stayed in Panam\u00e1 for around two weeks in January and February 2023 and visited three very different locations. The busy metropolis Panam\u00e1 City, the peaceful Santa Fe, and the adventurous Lost and Found hostel<\/a> between David and Bocas del Toro. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    The temperature on sea level was quite hot (Panam\u00e1 City) and much more moderate in the mountains (Santa Fe and Lost & Found). We mainly traveled by local and long-distance buses.<\/p>\n\n\n
