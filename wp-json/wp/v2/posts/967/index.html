When we were heading to Santa Elena (Piedechinche), we knew where to stay for a long time already, namely since our time in Costa Rica. That’s where we met Adam, a paraglider from Sweden who is working in Norway and spends half of the year in Colombia. His girlfriend Valentina is running an AirBnB<\/a> in town, so staying with them was an obvious choice. In particular, because I’ve got a new vario (flight computer) sent there after mine died a few weeks back.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

That gave me time to continue working on my Paraquip iOS App<\/a>. Specifically, I worked on making a concept called \u00bbwing load\u00ab easier to understand via data visualization and by providing guidance.
